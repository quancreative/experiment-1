Overview

Windwalker Corporation provides expertly tailored solutions in two main business areas: Training and Performance Management; and Research and Evaluation. We specialize in the analysis, design, development, delivery, and evaluation of targeted e-learning training products, in addition to bringing special expertise to the collection, analysis, and reporting of social science and educational research data.
